Program Overview

Research Training is a 3 months virtual program conducted by Nepal Research and Collaboration Center. The program aims to upskill research enthusiasts with necessary technical and non-technical knowledge necessary to conduct their research. Each session is facilitated by experienced trainers and supported by proficient mentors. The platform also gives an opportunity to build a strong network with researchers from diverse backgrounds.

This program offers:

  • Expert-led sessions
  • Interactive workshops
  • Collaborative projects
  • Networking opportunities
