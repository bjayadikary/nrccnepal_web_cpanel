    Let's Research Together

    Collaborate to make you dreams come true

    Nepal Research and Collaboration Center (NRCC) is a non-profit, non-governmental organization led by young minds to bring change to Nepal's research landscape. We began working in 2019 A.D. and were officially registered in 2022 A.D. We aim to be the bridge between national and international expert researchers and research enthusiasts, encouraging collaborative research. We have conducted various programs to create a research culture and connect people through research-based projects to achieve our goals.

    We strive to bring interdisciplinary fields of study under one roof through effective and collaborative learning. We aim to build a community that empowers Nepalese students with effective solutions and brings change to the education system. With programs like Monthly-Research Talk and three-month-long Research Training, we have been facilitating learning and growth platforms for Nepalese research enthusiasts. We have consistently promoted research throughout the country with research-based programs like Econ-Talk, Panel Discussion on Past, Present, and Future of Research in Nepal, NASA Spaceapps Kathmandu, and various STEM campaigns.

    Our Objectives

    At the Nepal Research and Collaboration Center, our primary objectives are to change Nepal's research landscape and develop collaborative and interdisciplinary research. By partnering with like-minded organizations and individuals, we aim to bridge the gap between research and practice, promoting an evidence-based approach to education.

    Research

    We partner between national and international researchers, creating a network that bridges the gap between research and practice through joint projects and initiatives.

    Innovate

    We promote a culture of learning by conducting programs that enhance research skills, empowering Nepalese students and professionals to contribute to global knowledge.

    Collaborate

    By integrating interdisciplinary approaches and innovative methodologies, we aim to innovative solutions that address educational challenges and improve the research landscape in Nepal and add value to scientific research worldwide.
