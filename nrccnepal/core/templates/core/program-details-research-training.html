3-Months-Long Research Training

  • Jan 1, 2022
  • 5 min read
Enhance your research skills nationwide with our online program led by diverse experts, cultivating an environment for intellectual growth.

Program Overview

Our three-month-long Research Training Program is a one-of-a-kind initiative in Nepal, conducted entirely online for accessibility across the nation. This exceptional program empowers participants to elevate their research skills through a comprehensive syllabus, with the guidance of experts in the field. The collaborative environment encourages active engagement among participants, allowing the exchange of ideas and the establishment of meaningful connections. The program occurs annually, bringing together a diverse group of participants to facilitate collaborative research opportunities.

Watch Overview Video

Syllabus Highlights

The comprehensive research training course covers essential topics such as research proposal writing, grant proposal writing, programming languages like Python, and tools like LaTeX. Additionally, participants will benefit from networking sessions with experts in the field, providing valuable insights and opportunities for collaboration.

Expert Trainers

Our program connects participants with over 20 expert trainers from various fields globally. These trainers are not only proficient educators but also accomplished researchers in their respective domains. The lectures, while generally covering a broad spectrum of domains, will be tailored for specific topics, ensuring participants receive specialized guidance from skilled professionals.

Dedicated Mentors

Complementing the training program, mentors from diverse domains are available to provide additional support. These mentors play a crucial role in enhancing participants' understanding of subject matters and catalyzing the development of their skills.

Empowering Nepalese Students

More than just a program, Research Training is a dream of empowering Nepalese students with unparalleled research opportunities. Together, we aim to unlock the potential within each participant, drive innovation, and make a lasting impact on society.

Join us in this transformative journey and be a part of shaping the future of research in Nepal. Together, we will unlock the limitless possibilities and foster a culture of excellence in research.
