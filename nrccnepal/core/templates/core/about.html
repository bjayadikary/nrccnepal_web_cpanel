Who are we?

Nepal Research and Collaboration Center (NRCC) is a non-profit organization founded in 2019 to foster a true learning culture in Nepalese society. We believe a true learing is facilitated by research-based and practical approach that provide students with the real world skills starting from an early age. We embrace the dynamic nature of learning process and are constantly challenging the status quo with our range of initiatives aimed at creating a space for the exchange of objectives among stakeholders in the Nepalese educational sector.

We also provide research opportunities, engage in advocacy and awareness-raising activities to promote research-based learning in Nepal and actively try to comprehend the problems faced by Nepalese students reaching out to them with the most efficient and practical solutions. Based in Kathmandu, we also reach out to the remotest areas of Nepal with our projects and programs. Our existence is for progressive institutionalized transformations at scale through collaboration of both institutions and individuals.

Our Mission & Vision

NRCC is on mission to provide students access to the opportunities, resources and support they need to establish a norm of true learning facilitated by research-based learning from an early age. Establishing a stable and trustworthy platform for the effective collaboration between the researchers from different disciplines, advocacy for the developments in the field of education, research and innovation is our top priority.

Nepal Research and Collaboration Center (NRCC) envisions a future where research-based learning is the norm in Nepalese society. Students have access to the opportunities, resources, and support they need to succeed, irrespective of their origins, ethnicity, gender, or socio-cultural status or even their field of study, enabling them to reach their optimum potential. Through our programs, we seek to empower every generations of Nepalese society with knowledge and skills that will enable them to succeed in an ever-changing world.

Chairman's Message

Welcome to the Nepal Research and Collaboration Center (NRCC)! Our center is dedicated to creating a future where research-based learning becomes the norm in Nepalese society. We believe that every student should have equal access to opportunities, resources, and support, regardless of their background or field of study.

At NRCC, our mission is to empower the future generations of Nepal by providing them with the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in an ever-changing world. Through our programs, we aim to establish a platform for true learning facilitated by research-based approaches from an early age.

We are committed to fostering effective collaboration between researchers from different disciplines, advocating for advancements in education, research, and innovation. By creating a stable and trustworthy platform, we aim to enable fruitful partnerships and meaningful collaborations among researchers, institutions, and organizations.

At NRCC, we are dedicated to continuous improvement and growth. We seek to enhance the evaluation system, administer examinations with excellence, and monitor academic programs, internships, and other relevant activities. Our commitment extends to fostering strong relationships with our team members and ensuring the smooth functioning of staff and training activities.

Join us in our journey towards empowering the future generation of researchers and making a lasting impact on the research landscape in Nepal.

Best regards,

Bibek Lal Karn
