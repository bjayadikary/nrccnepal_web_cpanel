Syllabus

Each section will be assigned an assignment that must be completed by the end of the week.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Research Training?

    Research training assumes paramount importance in the realm of science as it forms the bedrock for academic and professional accomplishments. In the context of Nepal, where scientific research is gaining traction, the significance of research training becomes even more pronounced. Our esteemed program offers students the unique prospect to cultivate indispensable skills encompassing experimental design, data collection, analysis, and interpretation. Armed with these invaluable proficiencies, participants can make substantial contributions to the scientific community by generating original knowledge and addressing pertinent local challenges in domains such as healthcare, agriculture, and environmental sustainability. However, the scarcity of resources, outdated curricula, and a scarcity of research-focused institutions often impede Nepalese students from accessing adequate research training opportunities. This stark disparity not only hampers their personal growth but also poses a formidable hurdle to the overall scientific progress of the nation, impeding their capacity to engage in rigorous and impactful research and curtailing their potential contributions to the wider scientific community.

  • What are its objectives?

    1. Unleash the Power of Data: Master data visualization and advanced analysis techniques for compelling insights.
    2. Amplify Your Research Impact: Communicate effectively and publish strategically to elevate your research.
    3. Forge Meaningful Connections: Join a vibrant research community for collaborations and opportunities.
    4. Ignite Your Research Journey: Build confidence and recognition as a skilled researcher.
    5. Embrace Inquiry: Explore diverse research strategies and drive groundbreaking discoveries
    6. Unlock Research Skills: Acquire valued expertise in literature studies, data synthesis, and analysis.
    7. Experience Transformative Learning: Engage in immersive activities to fuel your passion for research.

  • What are its expected outcomes?

    1. Enhanced Research Skills: Develop advanced skills in experimental design, data analysis, and interpretation.
    2. Interdisciplinary Collaboration: Engage with experts from diverse fields, fostering innovative collaborations.
    3. Creating Thinking: Cultivate a culture of innovation and explore novel research ideas.
    4. Effective Project Management: Acquire project management skills for effiecient research execution.
    5. Strong Communication: Improve presentation and dissemination skills for impactful research communication.
    6. Professional Networking: Expand your network and connect with researchers and industry professionals.
    7. Research Ethics and Integrity: Gain a solid understanding of ethical research practices

  • Who can apply?

    The training program is open to all individuals who have an interest in research and a desire to develop their skills and knowledge in a collaborative and supportive learning environment. Whether you are a student, professional, or researcher from any field, this training is designed to provide valuable insights and tools to enhance your research abilities.

  • Will I get a certificate upon completion?

    Yes, all the participants who complete the course will receive a certificate.

  • Who is NRCC?

    The Nepal Research and Collaboration Center (NRCC) is an esteemed institution dedicated to fostering a culture of research-based learning and academic excellence in Nepal. Since its inception in 2019, NRCC has been at the forefront of educational innovation, bringing together a vibrant community of educators, researchers, and learners to embark on a transformative journey of knowledge discovery and intellectual growth. At NRCC, we firmly believe that research-based learning is the key to unlocking individual potential and driving societal progress. Through our dynamic range of programs and initiatives, we empower students, educators, and professionals to delve into the realms of inquiry, critical thinking, and innovation. From our captivating "Read with NRCC" campaign, which instills a love for reading and lifelong learning, to our cutting-edge "Python for Research" training, equipping participants with invaluable data analysis and programming skills, we provide a rich and immersive learning environment that nurtures intellectual curiosity and ignites a passion for exploration. Join us at NRCC as we embark on a transformative educational journey that transcends boundaries and empowers individuals to make a meaningful impact in their fields of study. Together, we will push the boundaries of knowledge, foster collaboration and interdisciplinary research, and cultivate a new generation of thinkers and problem solvers who will shape the future of Nepal's scientific, academic, and social landscape. With NRCC, your educational aspirations will soar to new heights, as we strive to create a vibrant community of lifelong learners dedicated to making a difference in the world.
